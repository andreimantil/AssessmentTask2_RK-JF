Buttery Mashed Potatoes

INGREDIENTS

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Milk or cream

STEPS

  1. Cut potato into equal pieces.
  2. Boil in salted water until soft.
  3. Drain and mash with butter and milk or cream.
  4. Mash until creamy and fluffy.
  5. Transfer to a serving bowl and enjoy.
