Scrambled Eggs and Toast

INGREDIENTS

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Bread slices

STEPS

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with salt and milk.
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the egg mixture and stir gently.
  4. Cook until the eggs are just set.
  5. Toast the bread slices and serve with the scrambled eggs.
