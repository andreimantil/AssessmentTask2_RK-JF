Beef Burritos

INGREDIENTS

  • Beef Burrito Filling
  • Any type of rice
  • Corn
  • Black Beans
  • Cabbage or lettuce
  • Diced Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Cheese
  • Coriander/cilantro

STEPS

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat. Add garlic, onion, and beef. Cook until beef browns.
  2. Add taco seasoning, tomato paste, and water. Cook until water evaporates but beef remains juicy. Let it cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Place a tortilla on a work surface. Add rice, beef, lettuce, corn, black beans, tomato, red onion, coriander, and cheese.
  4. Fold the bottom of the tortilla over the filling. Fold the sides in tightly and roll up. Wrap in foil.
  5. To heat, pan fry in a dry pan or bake in foil at 180C/350F for 20 minutes. If refrigerated, it may take 30 minutes to warm through.
  6. Serve with sour cream, chilli sauce, salsa, or guacamole.
