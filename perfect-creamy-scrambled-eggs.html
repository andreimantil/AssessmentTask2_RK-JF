Perfect Creamy Scrambled Eggs

INGREDIENTS

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Heavy cream

STEPS

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with salt and heavy cream.
  2. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over low heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and stir gently.
  4. Cook slowly until eggs are soft and creamy.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy.
