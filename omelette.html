Omelette

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 eggs
  • Butter

STEPS

  1. Season beaten eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat until butter melts and foams.
  3. Pour eggs into the pan, let them cook for about 20 seconds, then use a spatula to make a line through the middle.
  4. Tilt the pan to fill it with the runny eggs. Repeat once or twice until the eggs are just set.
  5. Add your desired fillings like cheese, ham, herbs, mushrooms, or smoked salmon on top of the omelette.
  6. Gently fold the omelette in half with the spatula.
  7. Slide it onto a plate and serve.
